Welcome to Pansol Paradise

Nestled at the foot of Mount Makiling, Pansol Paradise Resort offers a unique natural hot spring experience that has made Calamba famous. Our resort harnesses the therapeutic mineral-rich waters that naturally flow from the mountain, providing our guests a relaxing and rejuvenating escape from city life.

Established in 2005, our family-owned resort has been a favorite destination for both locals and tourists seeking the healing benefits of Pansol's hot springs. With lush gardens and scenic mountain views, we provide a tranquil setting for your day trips and gatherings.

Location

Located in Barangay Pansol, Calamba City, Laguna. We are approximately 2 hours away from Manila via SLEX.

Directions:

From Manila, take SLEX and exit at Calamba. Follow the main road towards Pansol and look for our signage on the right side of the road, approximately 3 kilometers from the exit.
