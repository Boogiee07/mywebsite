Welcome to Laguna Hot Spring

Nestled at the foot of Mount Makiling, Laguna Hot Spring Resort offers a unique natural hot spring experience that has made Calamba famous. Our resort harnesses the therapeutic mineral-rich waters that naturally flow from the mountain, providing our guests a relaxing and rejuvenating escape from city life.

Established in 2005, our family-owned resort has been a favorite destination for both locals and tourists seeking the healing benefits of Laguna's hot springs. With lush gardens and scenic mountain views, we provide a tranquil setting for your day trips and gatherings.
